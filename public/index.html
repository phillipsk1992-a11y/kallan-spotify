woodshed
shedding, logged

A minimalist practice logger for musicians who want to show up every day. Track your sessions. Build streaks. See the work compound over time.

01 · log
One-tap practice logging

Pick a category, set your minutes, hit submit. No friction, no fuss. Technique, reading, chords, scales, tunes, theory — or define your own. Notes are optional. The goal is to make logging easier than skipping it.

02 · visualise
See your week at a glance

Seven days, colour-coded by intensity. Green means you showed up. The darker the square, the longer the session. Gaps are honest. Over time, the grid fills in and the pattern becomes the point.

4 sessions · 3 day streak · 2.1h/wk avg
03 · track
Streaks, stats, and the long game

Weekly totals, monthly breakdowns, year-long contribution grids. Watch where your time actually goes. The donut chart doesn't lie — and neither does a streak counter resetting to zero.

less more
04 · share
Share cards, not screenshots

Generate beautiful week, month, and year summary cards — donut charts, contribution grids, session counts — designed to be shared. Accountability through transparency. Post your week to your story or send it to your teacher.

05 · own
Your data, your practice

No account required. No social feed. No gamification tricks. Woodshed is a tool, not a platform. It works offline as a PWA, installs to your home screen, and keeps your practice log where it belongs — with you.
